Welcome Incoming Students!

Congrats! You've been chosen to become part of the best dorm on campus. We couldn't be more excited to welcome you to your new home, and we hope you're looking forward to it as much as we are. This isn’t just where you’ll live—it’s where you’ll grow, laugh, make unforgettable memories, and find a community that truly feels like home. Get excited for a warm welcome, incredible people, and the beginning of something truly special. We can’t wait to meet you and see everything you’ll bring to our community.

As you prepare for move in, check out the resources we've compiled below to help you get ready. Below, you'll find room information, packing lists, and other important details to help you make the most of your time in Coyle. Additionally, take some time to reach out to your roomate(s) and get to know them (you will be living together after all). Hopefully, you'll become good friends!
