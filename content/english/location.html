--- title: "Location" ---

A Legacy That Echoes From Quad To Quad.

The current home of the Coyle Community is nestled in the heart of campus, on the west side of North Quad. Only steps from the dome, LaFortune Student Center, North Dining Hall, and Hesburgh Library, Zahm's location is one of the best on campus. Whether its playing spikeball in front of Stonehenge on the quad, running around the nearby lakes, or stopping by The Huddle for a late night snack, Zahm Hall is close to nearly everything.

With the completion of the new Coyle Hall in Fall 2026, the dorm community will be moving back to its original home on South Quad, in the footprint of the old Fisher Hall.
