--- title: "Traditions" ---

YGR - Ya Gotta Regatta

St. Adalbert's

Coyle hall has been blessed with the opportunity to give back to the South Bend community by supporting St. Adalbert's Catholic School. Each year, the dorm donates to St. Adalbert's with funds raised from the Regatta, Car Smash, and other events. The Crew also volunteers at St. Adalbert's, helping with various events and activities, including tutoring, mentoring, and playing sports with the kids.

Coyle Formal - Spring

In the spring comes a classic Notre Dame dorm tradition, our formal. Usually co-hosted with a women's dorm, the Coyle Formal is a chance for the dorm to dress up and enjoy our own fancy night. The formal is held on campus and gives guys the opportunity to wear their best suits and ties and have a great time.

Coyle Funk - Fall

An iconic tradition that has been passed down from Fisher, Coyle Funk is our annual '70s themed SYR. Different from many other dorm SYRs (Screw Your Roommate), The Funk is a chance for everyone in Coyle, and their dates, to embrace their inner '70s spirit with groovy fashion, flare pants, disco shirts, and (for those particularly brave) handlebar mustaches and long sideburns. Each year, the dorm gathers in Stepan Center for a night of roller skating, dancing, and good old '70s fun.
